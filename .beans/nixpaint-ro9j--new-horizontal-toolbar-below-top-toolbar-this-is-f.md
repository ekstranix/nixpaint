Every tool will get their own settings and they will be available in the second horizonal toolbar. The top toolbar will contain main application buttons.

- paint tools: brush width, paint or stamp (click) mode. cycle rotate lambdas or one of 4 rotate states
- gum remove tool: gum size
- pan: no options
- node tool (draw lines from point to point): cycle rotate lambdas or one of 4 rotate states
- foreground color: palette: rainbow/blue/grayscale, stable/cycle
- background color: white, gray, current blue: checkbox: export background color
